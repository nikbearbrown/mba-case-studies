## A Note about AI

This book teaches the case interview as a discipline of structured thinking under pressure. Each chapter has its own *A note about AI* on the local framework. The unifying principle: **framework from the model, judgment from you.** The model can recite Porter's Five Forces, the BCG matrix, market entry checklists, and the rest of the canonical case vocabulary fluently. It cannot perform the test the interviewer is administering — structured reasoning under time pressure, defended live against an interviewer who is reading your composure. Use the model for drills, alternative framings, and pressure-testing your answers. The interview itself is yours, and the practice that prepares you for it is practice.
